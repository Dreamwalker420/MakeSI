Make Something of Yourself!

What is A Make-a-Thon? ...

A make-a-thon is a two-day campus event where students collaborate to design, engineer, and present ideas with the assistance of mentors to compete for prizes and glory!

MakeSI 2016 Schedule

Mark your calendars for April 2nd & 3rd, 2016 for MakeSI.

The event center will be open from 9:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m. Saturday and Sunday. Event participants will be treated to meals and snacks onsite so you can focus your efforts on creation. There will also be tutorial workshops if you want to learn more about design and engineering.
